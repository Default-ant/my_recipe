Ingredients:

    1 Banana
    dark chocolate 40 -50 grams
    cocoa powder 3 tablespoons
    protein powder 1 scoop(chocolate flavour if possible)
    oats 70g
    baking powder
    chocolates syrup 30g
    2eggs
    milk 70ml
    ghee 2 tablespoons

Steps to make:

    blend or mix eggs and milk
    add remaining ingredients and blend them
    put butter or ghee to baking pan
    pour the batter into pan
    put the pan in air fryer
    bake them for 20min with 180c
