Ingredients:

  • 1 Banana
  • dark chocolate 40 -50 grams
  • cocoa powder 3 tablespoons
  • protein powder 1 scoop(chocolate flavour if possible)
  • oats 70g
  • baking powder
  • chocolates syrup 30g
  • 2eggs
  • milk 70ml
  • ghee 2 tablespoons

Steps to make:

  1. blend or mix eggs and milk
  2. add remaining ingredients and blend them
  3. put butter or ghee to baking pan
  4. pour the batter into pan
  5. put the pan in air fryer
  6. bake them for 20min with 180c
